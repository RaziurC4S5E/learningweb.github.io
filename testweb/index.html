Chelsea Football Club

Chelsea Football Club is an English professional football club based in Fulham, West London.

List of Chelsea players

Founded in 1905, the club competes in the Premier League, the top division of English football. Domestically, the club has won six league titles, eight FA Cups, five League Cups, and four FA Community Shields.
